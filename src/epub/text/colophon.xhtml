The Kingdom of God Is Within You
was published in YEAR by
Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
David M. Gross.
It is based on Leo Wiener’s translation (1905).
The editors of this edition have made a few improvements, for instance where Tolstoy quotes from English-language sources for which we were able to find the originals, but that Wiener translated back from Tolstoy’s own Russian translations. We have also offered translations of some foreign phrases, which appear in [brackets].

The cover page is adapted from
Leo Tolstoy Barefoot,
a painting completed in 1901 by
Ilya Repin.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
